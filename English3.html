Shahraystvo with cryptocurrency

The organizers of the fraudulent scheme often choose little-known cryptocurrencies (but this not means that it is also not possible to do with the famous, it's just that scammers need more money for this. – Ed.) and start buying little by little, in order to this the process did not affect the market price. The second stage is the Pump itself. Fraudsters begin to distribute "exclusive" information about what this one the coin is about to increase in value. This is done through a huge number of "expert" Telegram channels, groups in social networks or bloggers. The main thing is create the illusion of asset growth. So that the statements are not empty, fraudsters also begin to buy cryptocurrency so that it starts to grow in prices If the number of those who believed the information and want to get rich instantly is sufficient, the cryptocurrency is real starts become more expensive, because many victims of fraudsters begin to buy it, thereby pushing the price up. But here comes the Dump stage. Fraudsters instantly sell all their coins and the price goes down. More often even lower, than was before pump. Often the victims of this scheme know that they are participating in a Pump&Dump, but hope that they will have time to sell theirs coins in time, after all, the fraudsters promised to warn about the date of the "downpour". But fraudsters are also fraudsters to deceive. Interestingly, Pump&Dump can only take hours or a few minutes if the cryptocurrency is relatively cheap. The history of cryptocurrency creation.

Back in the 1960s, professional cryptographers discussed the possibility creation of global information network The first practical steps in this direction were taken in the 80s. They started with the help of the information network exchange brokerage data, which were required for trading on exchanges.

But people have been looking at cryptocurrency for quite a long time, the idea of virtual money was quite strange and uninspiring trust However, the new currency gradually gained popularity and increased its value. If at the dawn of its existence by one bitcoin could be used for a meal in a restaurant, now it can be used to buy a small apartment or a house in the suburbs Such a rapid growth became the reason for the increased demand and interest of people in cryptocurrency.

Cryptography for the purpose of confidential payments began to be used since 1990 David Chom's DigiCash system. Cryptography is the practice of transferring data between two or more parties. Its peculiarity is that these there is a message fully encrypted and secure. Because of this, cryptocurrency transactions are confidential and do not require banking mediators.
However, its payment system was centralized, and for the first time the term "cryptocurrency" began to be used after its appearance peer-to-peer Bitcoin payment system, which was developed in 2009 by a person or group of persons under the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto. Later, other Bitcoin-independent cryptocurrencies, called Bitcoin forks, appeared: Namecoin, Litecoin, Scrypt, Novacoin, PPCoin.
