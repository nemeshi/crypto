History of creation

Long before the creation of Bitcoin in 1983,
David Chaum and Stefan Brands proposed the first
"electronic cash" protocols

A little about David Chaum

David Chaum (Full David Lee Chaum) is an
American computer scientist and
cryptographer. He is known for the development
of e-cash, the purpose of which is to preserve the anonymity
of the user.

When was cryptocurrency created?

It is difficult to say in which year the crypto was created,
but there is a first publication describing the principles
of the blockchain and its protocol, it happened
in 2008, author - Satoshi Nakamoto

A little about Satoshi Nakamoto

Satoshi Nakamoto is the pseudonym of the person or
group of people who developed the
Bitcoin cryptocurrency protocol
and created the first version of the software,
that implemented this protocol.
Several attempts have been made to uncover the real person
or group behind the name, but none have been successful.
